## RECORDING NOTES:

### DELIVERY STYLE:
- **Conversational but intense** - like you're talking to a friend who keeps making the same mistake
- **Don't hide your accent** - it's proof the system works
- **Pause strategically** - let painful truths sink in
- **Vary pace** - slow down on pain points, speed up on urgency

### TONE BY SECTION:
- **Hook**: Sharp, attention-grabbing
- **Pain articulation**: Empathetic but honest (you've been there)
- **Agitation**: Frustrated friend telling hard truths
- **Solution**: Confident, matter-of-fact
- **CTA**: Direct, no-nonsense

### BACKGROUND AUDIO:
- Subtle tension-building music
- NO music during deepest pain points (too manipulative)
- Slight uptick in energy during CTA

### WHICH VERSION TO USE:
- **Version 1** (Funnel Graveyard): Best for cold traffic, broad appeal
- **Version 2** ($3,564 Mistake): Best for people researching ClickFunnels
- **Version 3** (Tutorial Treadmill): Best for YouTube pre-roll, tutorial watchers
- **Version 4** (Midnight Realization): Best for retargeting, warmer audience
- **Version 5** (Comparison Trap): Best for serial course buyers, info junkies
